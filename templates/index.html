About Instructor Yaseen

Good Drive is a personalized driving training service based in Ernakulam, offering expert instruction with a calm, confidence-building approach.

I’m Yaseen, an Automobile Engineer and professional driving instructor specializing in driving anxiety and hypnotic driving training. This unique technique combined with my experience as a driving instructor makes me the go-to person for help with a fear of driving.

Many people worry that there is no way of fixing driving anxiety. You can overcome driving anxiety in as little as an hour! Fixing a fear of driving is as simple as releasing stress and pressure from your life and accepting that how you were does not have to be how you are. If you want to change and overcome the problem you can, quite quickly!

If you want to learn more contact me to get support.
